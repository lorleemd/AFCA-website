Loretta L. Lee, MD

Dr. Loretta Lee obtained her medical degree from Tufts University School of Medicine in Boston. She has been in practice for over 20 years specializing in Internal Medicine. She treats various conditions including thyroid disorders, hormone replacement therapy, diabetes management, women’s health and much more. Dr. Lee is a dedicated and devoted provider that is able to accommodate most patient’s needs.

Ulyana Stiassny, MD

Dr. Ulyana Stiassny was born and raised in Kamchatka Peninsula. She received her medical education and degree from the Khabarovsk State Medical School, graduating in 1996. She moved to Anchorage in 1998, and enrolled in the Alaska Family Practice Residency in 2005 receiving her Board Certification in Family Medicine.

For the past 18 years her practice has focused on broad based family medicine with concentration on preventive care for all ages, and women's health care including menopause management and broad based chronic medical management.

A mother of two (2), Dr. Stiassny enjoys her family time, dinner with friends, gardening, loves to ski, ice skate, hike, and walk her dog Buzz.
