Pediatrics Care:

Kid’s Corner

Our children’s services include:

  • Well child checks
  • Acute care for illnesses of childhood
  • Immunizations

We have experienced nursing staff who can answer many questions. They can help you work through problems and decide if an appointment is necessary, or if you can provide care for your child at home.

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention also offers a great Parent Portal, covering everything from safety at home and the community to immunization schedules and developmental milestones.

We also recommend the CDC Immunization Catch-Up Scheduler. This is a downloadable service that can be used to determine the vaccines your child needs, and is especially useful for quickly seeing missed or skipped vaccines.
