Bailie Nieder, Manager

Bailie was born in Arizona and raised in Alaska. She has worked for Alaska Family Care Associates since the age of 16. She first started in medical records and is now a key part to running a successful practice. Bailie obtained her Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from the University Of Anchorage Alaska. She is a true Alaskan who enjoys camping, fishing, hunting and hiking.
